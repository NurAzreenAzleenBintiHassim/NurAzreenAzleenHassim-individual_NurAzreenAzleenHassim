My Education

My journey in education.



Kindergarten (Tabika Kemas)


I started my early education in 2006 at a government kindergarten, Tabika Kemas located in Simpang Lima. At that time I was only five years old. I attended the kindergarten for two years from 2006 to 2007 when I was six years old. During those two years, I learned various basic knowledge, especially the knowledge that I needed the year before entering primary school such as recognizing letters and numbers, spelling, and basic reading.

Primary School (Sk Sri Kerian)


In 2008 I started my first year in elementary school. I went to school at a rather famous school called Sekolah Kebangsaan Sri Kerian, which was established in 1877 as Sekolah Melayu Parit Buntar. This school is one of the schools that obtained very excellent results in the UPSR examination. The school is a bit far from my house however I go and come back to school sent by my family members. I went to school there for six years from 2008 to 2013. I learned a lot of new knowledge and made many new friends who came from various places. It gave me many sweet memories and important life lessons. I was also a Resource Center Supervisor for three years and I had to run errands twice a week during breaks at the school resource center. In 2013 I took the UPSR exam and obtained a result of 3A 2B.

High School (Smk Panglima Bukit Gantang)


I started my schooling at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Panglima Bukit Gantang in 2014, The school took its name in conjunction with the name of the first Perak minister, Dato 'Panglima Bukit Gantang. At that time I was 13 years old. At the school there are two afternoon and morning school sessions. Due to my good UPSR results I was placed in the morning session school in the Special Plan class where this class has an Arabic language class that must be taken by all who enter the class up to form 3. In 2017 I have to choose the form 4 class stream available in my school . In my school there are seven streams namely science, account, religion, technique, literature, vocational and art. I chose the technique class because I wanted to study the subject of engineering drawing. I took the class until I was in Form 5 in 2018. At that time I took a very important exam, SPM.

Higher Education (UiTM)


After graduating from secondary school, I continued my studies at a higher level at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Kedah Branch. I started my studies at UiTM in 2019 at the diploma level. I took an information management course that contained five semesters and I will graduate in February 2022. During my studies at UiTM I learned various new skills and lessons. I was also able to make new friends who came from various states. I was able to learn a lot of new cultures and things from other states. I was also able to improve my social skills and learn knowledge especially in better time and self management.

My Experiences

Interesting Experience I've Ever Had.



Climb Bukit Limau


In 2019, I did some climbing activities for the first time. The hill that is my choice to climb with my friends is Bukit Limau which is located in Permatang Kerat Telunjuk, Bandar Baharu, Kedah. Although it is quite difficult to find Bukit Limau because it is located in the interior but it is very worth it. This is because the cozy atmosphere and natural beauty that is on the hill is very beautiful. Bukit Limau got its name because it is surrounded and along the way to climb the hill there will be various types of lime trees such as Limau Nipis, Limau Kasturi, Limau Purut and many more. This experience is something new for me and I hope I can do this activity again in the future.



World Water Camp


In March 2020 my friend and I participated in a camp organized by the Perak state ministry. This camp was held in conjunction with the Perak State Level World Water Day celebration. The camp lasted for 2 days and 1 night at Hotel Casuarina and Kledang Saiong Forest Eco Park. We had the opportunity to meet the Perak minister and the environment minister face to face, this was a very meaningful opportunity for me. This camp gathers all students who come from various universities and polytechnics throughout Malaysia. Throughout the camp we learned 7 water -related learning modules. Each module will be guided by speakers coming from various water-related agencies.



Theater KESEDA


In my first year at UiTM Kedah I joined an arts and culture club. This club is known as KESEDA. In 2020 the KESEDA club has organized a theater entitled Hajat. This theater is one of the biggest events ever performed by the KESEDA club. My friend and I were directly involved in this biggest event. We were assigned as a behind -the -scenes crew, where we were tasked to ensure all agendas ran smoothly and manage the group of spectators from schools near UiTM who were present to watch this theater. This experience is one that I will never forget in a lifetime, this is because it is one of the rare experiences I will participate in. I also met many new friends who came from various courses and semesters.
